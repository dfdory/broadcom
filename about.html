About Us

Who we are?

We are a team of renowned experienced Telecom, IT, Electrical and Civil Engineering Professionals.
BroadCom is relatively new players, however many of its Team members have managed the Planning, Rollout and Operations of fixed/mobile Telecommunication infrastructures in Cameroon and Central Africa over the past two Decades and are holders of related professional certifications. They have greatly contributed to the success of various organizations they have worked for and were able to lead them in achieving very high quality standard.
The team is highly motivated and considers the voice of the customer as the company main driver in its endeavor to build the Digital Services Landscape of tomorrow for Cameroon and Central Africa sub-region.
